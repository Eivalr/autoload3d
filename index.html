How AutoLoad 3D Works

01

Input Cargo

Add items manually or via CSV. The cargo list shows as a table for full overview of all shipments, dimensions, and stacking rules.

02

Auto Algorithm

Runs LAFF, DBLF, and Layer Builder automatically. Picks the best result by utilization. Switch between algorithms with one click.

03

Realistic Constraints

Stacking priority, crush limits, COG checks, and weight distribution enforced — not just volume optimization.

04

All Equipment

Auto-selects optimal equipment, or choose Air ULDs, sea containers, trucks with axle limits, or custom equipment.

05

3D Visualization

Interactive viewer per container — drag, zoom, wireframe, explode, shipment ID painted on every box face.

06

Save & Export

Save loadings to your account. Export PDF with step-by-step sequence, CSV loading list, PNG snapshots, and printable Guided Loading.
