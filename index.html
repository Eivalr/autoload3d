How AutoLoad 3D Works

01

Input Cargo

Add items manually or via CSV. Each item carries dimensions, weight, shipment reference, stackability, and rotation rules.

02

Three Algorithms

LAFF, DBLF, and Layer Builder each solve the packing problem differently. Compare all three and pick the best result.

03

Realistic Constraints

Stacking priority, crush limits, category rules, weight distribution, and COG checks are all enforced.

04

All Equipment Types

Air ULDs with accurate contoured shapes, sea containers, trucks with axle weight awareness, and custom equipment.

05

3D Visualization

Interactive Three.js viewer with rotation, zoom, wireframe, explode mode, and shipment ID printed on every box.

06

Guided Loading

Step-by-step printable instructions for warehouse staff with sequence numbers, exact positions, and rotation callouts.
