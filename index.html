How AutoLoad 3D Works

01

Input Your Cargo

Add items manually or upload a CSV. Each item carries its dimensions, weight, shipment reference, and stacking rules.

02

Smart Container Selection

The app automatically picks the smallest suitable container. No manual selection needed. If cargo overflows, it calculates how many containers are required.

03

Realistic Stacking Logic

Items are sorted by weight and stack priority. Heavy, strong items go first. Fragile items are never crushed. Rotation is only applied when allowed.

04

3D Visualization

Rotate, zoom, and inspect your load in real-time 3D. Each shipment is color-coded for instant visual distinction.
